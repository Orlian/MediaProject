Frequently Asked Questions

  • Kolumbus was designed to alleviate the solitude of Coronavirus isolation. With Kolumbus you can visit sights near and far in a form of a game!
    Discover new sights both near and far.

  • Starting is very simple! Just head to the map page (icon with an arrow to 4 different directions), view your first hint and start the timer!
    The hint will tell you which sight you need to head next!

  • Absolutely! On the main page, you can add your YouTube username to the box "Youtube-Username", then click the green button.
    This loads the playlists that you have stored publically and allows you to listen to one of them.
    Just choose your favourite playlist from the dropdown box, and hit the grey button. Now you can start listening to your favourite songs!

  • It is not mandatory to use the timer, but if you wish to make this a competition for yourself, you can start the timer when viewing the hint and time how long it takes you to solve it!
